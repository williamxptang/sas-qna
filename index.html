Inside the tool — three layers, stacked

  1. Knowledge layer — School profile (one per school), platform standards (XHS · WeChat rules), brand assets (logo · colors · fonts · BGM). This is what your answers below build.
  2. Chat layer — One assistant you talk to. It confirms what you want, runs it, shows the result. Your materials drop in here.
  3. Pipeline layer — Five modes the assistant picks from: t2t (write text), t2i (create image), i2i (edit image), t2v (short video), video edit (cut your footage). Output lands in output/<school>/YYMMDD/, ready to post.
